FAQ

What are the restaurant's opening hours?

We are open from Tuesday to Sunday, 5:00 PM to 11:00 PM.

How can I make a reservation?

Reservations can be made by calling our reservation line at (555) 123-4567 or through our online booking system.

Are walk-ins accepted?

Walk-ins are welcome, but we recommend making a reservation to secure your spot.

Is there a dress code?

Smart casual attire is recommended for a delightful dining experience.

Do you cater to dietary restrictions?

Yes, our chefs are happy to accommodate dietary restrictions. Please inform us when making a reservation.
